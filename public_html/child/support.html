Can I make changes to my pre-order?

To change your pre-order payment or delivery details, without cancelling your order, follow the steps below.

1. Select ‘My Account‘

2. Select ‘My Orders‘

3. Find the order and click ‘Order Details‘

4. Click ‘Change Payment details‘ or ‘Change delivery address‘

5. Amend details

6. Click ‘Update‘

If you do not have a GAME account please contact our Customer Service team.

Please Note: The product(s) on a pre-order cannot be changed once it has been submitted, however, you are able to place a new order through the website with the correct product(s).

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before preparation of dispatch has begun. Just log in to ‘My Account’, and follow the steps below;

1. Click “My Orders”

2. Locate the order you would like to cancel

3. Click “Order details”

Beneath the product, you would like to cancel, click “Cancel This Product” (BEWARE: Clicking this button will immediately cancel your order)

Please note: once your order has been prepped for dispatch, it is not possible to cancel using this process, however, you can still return it within 30 days for a refund or exchange, as long as the seal remains unbroken – Click here for more details on our goodwill 30-day return policy.

You also have you have the right to cancel your order for any item bought on this website for a full refund under the Consumer Contracts (information, Cancellation and Additional Charge)s Regulations. Click here for more details on how to exercise this right of cancellation. If your order was made as a guest you will need to contact our Customer Service Team to cancel your order.

3 May, 2016 by Hz3

What happens if my order has not arrived?

Before contacting GAME to report a lost order or late delivery, please follow the steps below:

1. Have you checked on the status of your order using our Order Tracking system?

2. Has it been 5 working days, not including Sundays (or 21 days for international orders) since your order was despatched? See our Terms and Conditions of sale.

Please note with Pre-orders we need to allow 10 working days from release date.

In the unlikely event that your order has been lost in transit, we will do our best to replace it as soon as possible (depending upon product availability). Please be aware that you must contact us within 60 days of the despatch date of your order if it hasn’t arrived via the postal service. Unfortunately claims for lost in transit orders after this time cannot be accepted.

What Next?

If the required number of working days has elapsed please contact Customer Services.

Special Deliveries

Orders shipped via courier require the customer’s signature upon delivery and cannot be released by the driver without this.
